Cookie Recipe

By: Samuel Solis

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Sugar

Instructions:

  1. Mix Everything
  2. Store in the fridge for 2 hours
  3. Make Balls of dough and place evenly in a cooking pan with parchment paper
  4. Bake for 15 minutes or until golden brown

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Serves: 10 people

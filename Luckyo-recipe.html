Homemade Einsteins Bagel

By: Lucky Onyedebelu

Ingredients:

  • 1 Einstein Bros. Take & Toast Plain Bagel
  • 2 Farmhouse Eggs Cage Free Large Brown Eggs
  • 3 slices of Great Value Hickory Smoked Bacon
  • Great Value Sharp Cheddar Finely Shredded Cheese 32 oz
    OR Kraft Sharp Cheddar Shredded Cheese
  • 1 Pillsbury Cinnamon Roll (from the Pillsbury Grands! cinnamon roll tube)

Instructions:

Optional: Prepare Cinnamon Roll

  1. Open the Pillsbury Cinnamon Roll tube by peeling the wrapper and pressing the seam with a spoon.
  2. Place the cinnamon rolls on a baking sheet, spaced apart.
  3. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10–12 minutes or until lightly golden.
  4. Spread the included icing over the warm rolls before serving.

Bagel Sandwich Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) if you plan to warm the cinnamon roll.
  2. Cook 3 slices of Great Value Hickory Smoked Bacon in a skillet until crispy. Set aside.
  3. Scramble or fry the 2 Farmhouse Eggs Cage Free Large Brown Eggs to your preference.
  4. Slice and toast the Einstein Bros. Take & Toast Plain Bagel lightly until golden.
  5. Place the cooked eggs on the bottom half of the toasted bagel.
  6. Add the cheddar cheese (Great Value Sharp Cheddar Shredded OR Kraft Sharp Cheddar Shredded) so it melts slightly on top of the warm eggs.
  7. Layer the crispy bacon strips over the melted cheese.
  8. Place the top half of the bagel on to complete the sandwich.
  9. Serve with the cinnamon roll on the side.

Time & Servings:

Cooking time: ~15–20 minutes

Serves: 1

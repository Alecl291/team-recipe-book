Authentic Shawarma by Hamza Shakur

By: Hamza Shakur (EUID: 0062)

Ingredients (serves 4):

  • 1 kg chicken thigh fillets
  • 5 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp paprika, 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Pita bread, garlic sauce, pickles, fries

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken overnight in all spices + yogurt
  2. Stack on vertical rotisserie or grill pan
  3. Shave thin slices as it cooks
  4. Wrap in pita with garlic sauce & pickles
  5. Serve with fries & extra garlic sauce

Cooking time: 45 min | Difficulty: 4/5 | Serves: 4
